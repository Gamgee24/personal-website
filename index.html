my name is sam. welcome to my personal site. i am based in stockholm and i like long runs.

i'm obsessed with endurance in the outdoors. i spend much of my time ultrarunning and working with people exploring performance and expanding what humans can do.

i've spent eight years operating and investing in early-stage technology companies, and am now working with and learning from companies, researchers and athletes at the frontier of outdoor culture, endurance and performance.

i care deeply about wild spaces, the power they hold, and how feats of endurance in these places change how we understand ourselves and the world around us. i write about some of this at Suffer Together.

i also work selectively with runners on training, racing and athlete mindset. please get in touch below if you'd like to discuss working together.
